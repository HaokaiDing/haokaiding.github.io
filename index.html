About Me个人简介

I am an undergraduate in Electronic Science and Technology at Shenzhen Technology University. Concurrently, as an X Scholar in a joint program with Tsinghua University and Shenzhen X-Institute, I work under the guidance of Prof. Wenzeng Zhang. My research focuses on underactuated robotic grippers and novel mechanism design, with specific interests in idle-stroke mechanisms, Peaucellier-based linkages, and adaptive grasping strategies. I have published 8 papers in 2025 at venues including IROS, CASE, and Polymers (JCR Q1), and served as an IROS 2025 Session Chair. 我是深圳技术大学电子科学与技术专业本科生，同时作为清华大学钱学森班–深圳零一学院零一学者，在张文增教授指导下开展研究。我的研究方向为欠驱动机器人抓手与新型机构设计，具体涉及空行程机构、佩索利耶连杆及自适应抓取策略。2025 年已在 IROS、CASE、Polymers（JCR Q1）等发表 8 篇论文，并担任 IROS 2025 分会主席。

Research Interests研究方向

Underactuated Grippers 欠驱动抓手

Peaucellier linkages, idle-stroke mechanisms, and adaptive grasping strategies for dexterous manipulation with minimal actuators. 基于佩索利耶连杆与空行程机构的自适应抓取策略，以最少驱动实现灵巧操作。

SpiRobs (Spiral Soft Robots) 螺旋软体机器人 (SpiRobs)

Continuum robots with spiral architecture for confined-space navigation, integrating suction capabilities for enhanced interaction. 基于螺旋结构的连续体机器人，融合吸附功能实现狭窄空间导航与增强交互。

Aerial Manipulation 空中操作

UAV perching mechanisms with electromagnetic adhesion systems for stable attachment and manipulation on varied surfaces. 基于电磁吸附的无人机降落与操作机构，实现多种表面的稳定附着。
